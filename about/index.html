About

I am a Ph.D. student at University of Genoa, Italy with Mechanics, Robotics and Measurements curriculum , Italy. I majored in Advanced Robotics from an Erasmus Mundus program named European Master on Advanced Robotics+ with dual degrees from Ecole Centrale de Nantes, France and University of Genoa, Italy. I obtained my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering with Gold Medal from Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College, India..

While awaiting my admittance to master’s program aboard, I worked as Assistant System Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services, India developing Java based API for The Home Depot, USA.

My vision “Robotics as a tool for better understanding of the world and beyond”. I love developing custom robotics solutions for any problem. Simply put, automate ethically, effectively and efficiently with the robotic tools I have, and I will learn. I also like creating virtual reality applications for visualization purposes.

I have been more satisfied with my career since I started pondering two simple questions at the end of the day, “What did I learn?” and “What did I contribute”.

Currently, I am focusing on developing a customized motion planning algorithm and mechanics for a 3RSS motion platform SP7 Motion simulator.
