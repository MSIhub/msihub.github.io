About me

I am from Puducherry, India. A union territory renowned formally for high-quality medical educational institutes and informally for weekend parties with beautiful beaches and resorts at every knock and corner. I traveled to Nantes, France, for my master's degree, and then to Genoa, Italy. My journey to gain more knowledge is continuing. I enjoy discussing about technology, physics, and the possibilities for improving one's quality of life with it.

In May 2021, I defened my Ph.D. thesis on "Motion generation and planning for Robotic and VR based motion simulator" at University of Genoa, Italy with Mechanics, Robotics and Measurements curriculum , Italy. I am currently working as a post-doctoral researcher with the University of Genoa to continue my research on effective parametric reduction for motion cueing algorithms. I majored in Advanced Robotics from an Erasmus Mundus program named European Master on Advanced Robotics+. I obtained my Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering with Gold Medal from Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College, India.. I worked as Assistant System Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services, India developing Java based API for The Home Depot, USA. You can download my CV here.

Currently, I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Genoa, Italy working on motion cueing strategies and algorithms to enhance the immersion in virtual reality using a motion simulator. I have developed end-to-end solutions for conducting experiments on a virtual reality motion simulator. In parallel to my research, working on learning skills for professional virtual reality prototyping using Unity. Have a look at my projects page for some videos on my research work.
