About me

I'm Mohamed Sadiq Ikbal, deeply immersed in the world of Autonomous Systems and Robotics. My expertise is rooted in C++, Python, Linux, Docker, ROS, and GIT, which I have leveraged in various innovative projects. My role at STTech GmbH in Munich, Germany, has me engaged in the full-stack development and deployment of complex features in AI and robotics systems, showcasing my technical proficiency and problem-solving skills.

Throughout my career, starting from an Assistant System Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services to my current position, I have continually honed my technical abilities. My academic journey, culminating in a Ph.D. in Mechanics, Measurement, and Robotics from the University of Genoa, Italy, has provided me with a solid foundation in both the theoretical and practical applications of AI and robotics.

In my professional path, I've focused on integrating advanced research with practical development to create effective solutions. This blend of academic insight and hands-on experience has been crucial in my contributions to the field of AI and robotics.

Beyond my technical endeavors, I am an avid learner and enjoy sharing knowledge. This passion extends to my personal interests in hiking, badminton, and exploring diverse cultures through travel. These pursuits reflect my belief in a balanced approach to life, where personal growth and professional development go hand in hand
